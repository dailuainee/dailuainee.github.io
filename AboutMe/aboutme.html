About Me

My Career Aspirations


My desire to pursue a career in the computer science industry had begun quite recently. Upon graduating high school as a valedictorian, my mind was not set on which career pathway I should take -- until I came across the YouTube influecers Chunbuns and Mayuko. I felt inspired by their videos to become an influential female figure in tech myself, so I decided to major in computer science at UNLV.

My Devotion to Community Building


Ever since joining the National Junior Honors Society during my middle school years, I have been devoted to volunteering for the Las Vegas community. During high school, I completed 200+ hours of community service for different organizations in Las Vegas, and eventually earned the President’s Service Award in 2019. Currently, under my scholarship program, I am volunteering for the Public Education Foundation where I’m working to complete 300+ service hours before graduation.
